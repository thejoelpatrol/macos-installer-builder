fuseHFS: a macFUSE filesystem module for Mac OS Standard (HFS) filesystems on macOS 12

!! IMPORTANT: fuseHFS requires macFUSE to be installed.
Download macFUSE here.

This version is tested to work with macFUSE 4.4.0 on x86-64 and macOS 12 Monterey. ARM (Apple Silicon) likely works through Rosetta but is not natively supported yet.
